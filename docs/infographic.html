Governance in Action

Proposal Quorum

To ensure that decisions represent the community's will, a proposal must meet a 10% quorum of the total token supply. This requirement ensures legitimacy and prevents small groups from passing proposals without broader support.

The Proposal Lifecycle

Each proposal follows a transparent and structured timeline to allow for community discussion, thoughtful voting, and secure execution of the outcome.

1

Submit Proposal

A community member submits a new project idea for funding.

2

Voting Period (7 Days)

The community votes on the proposal using their GreenTokens.

3

Execution

If passed, funds are released from the treasury to the project.
